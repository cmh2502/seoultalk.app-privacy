Privacy Policy for SeoulTalk

Last updated: 2025-10-29

SeoulTalk ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the SeoulTalk mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Email Address: Collected for user authentication and account management.

1.2 Location Information

  • Country Information: Used to provide region-based services.
  • Device Location Information: Collected only while the App is in use to support core functionality.

1.3 User Communications

  • Chat Messages: Messages sent through the in-app chat feature are stored to enable message delivery, conversation history, and customer support. Chat messages are automatically deleted after 30 days.

1.4 User-Generated Images

  • Images and Photos: Images uploaded via bulletin boards or chat are stored to enable content sharing and communication. Uploaded images are automatically deleted after 30 days, or earlier if deleted by the user.

1.5 Information We Do Not Collect

  • Contact lists
  • Audio files
  • Payment or financial information

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate users and manage accounts
  • To provide and maintain App functionality
  • To support user communication features
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information. Information may be disclosed only when required by law or to protect users and the public.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect user data. Data transmitted between the App and our servers is protected using industry-standard encryption.

5. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. Chat messages and uploaded images are deleted after 30 days.
